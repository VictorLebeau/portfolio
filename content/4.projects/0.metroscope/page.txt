title: Métroscope
-
date: 2010, October–
-
content:
For the artistic event La Nuit blanche, in 2010, in Paris a group of students of Ensad had the chance to create an event in the abandoned subway station Saint-Martin. We created costumes and postures that remain letters from the word "Saint-Martin".
